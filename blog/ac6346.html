Thoughtcrimes

As well as you are not allowed to take someone else's life, you are not allowed to force someone else to live.

Free will is a myth. Things just do happen, for whatever reason, and there is no assumed atomic "you" to control even a bit of anything in a first place.

The only purpose of any kind of cruelty is to satisfy bloodlust. All other justifications are made up.

Failure is always an option, you can never count on or vouch for anything with absolute certainty.

No one will ever figure out everything, handling uncertainty right is the best ever skill to develop.

Power of authority is never external.
